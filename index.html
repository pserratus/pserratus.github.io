Completed Projects

ASVZ ETH Zürich Gym*
*The ASVZ offers all university members in Zürich an attractive and varied sports program, which offers plenty of opportunities to discover new activities but also includes old favorites.

With corona around the Asvz introducced a new policy where you had to book courses in a spesific time-frame and place. That ment that students had to manually go and book it. Most people that used the sport center frecuently forgot about booking a spot or missed the time-frame. With WhatsApp-Bridge they could send a message spesifying the course they wanted, and when the course would go online the program would automatically enroll them and send them a notification with the confirmation. Poeple ended up enrolling through WhatsApp more than going to the Asvz app and even if they didn't need the speed to enroll, they used WhatsApp anyways as it was quicker and more convinient for them.
